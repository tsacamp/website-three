Deep time

One of my favorite parts of the dino hall was the deep time exhibt. It showed dinos from the mesozoic era. They showed the torosaurus, the t-rex, the triceratops, and the ceratasaurus. Deep time also didnt have just dinosaurs, they also had animals from the ice age such as the wolly mamoth skeleton, which is placed at the enterance of the deep time exhibit.

Fossils

My all time favorite part of the hall was definitely the fossils. I loved watching the workers clean the fossils, and look at them under the microscope. Right next to the big window was an interactive game where you had to be a old animal and you had to choos ewhere you were going to live and depending on what animal you choose and what climate you choose to live in, you would either fossilize, decompose, or you weould get eaten, if you were a triceratops near a water climate, you might decompose.
